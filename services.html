Our Specialized Services

Tailored rehabilitation programs designed to meet your unique needs.

Home Physiotherapy Services

We understand that mobility issues can make visiting a clinic difficult. Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit brings premium physiotherapy care directly to your home in Ahmedabad. This service is designed to provide maximum convenience without compromising on the quality of treatment.

Who is it for?

  • Elderly patients with limited mobility.
  • Patients recovering from major surgeries (Joint replacements, Spine surgery).
  • Neurological patients (Stroke, Parkinson's).
  • Anyone preferring the privacy and comfort of home.

Online Physiotherapy Consultation

Access expert care from anywhere in the world. Our online physiotherapy programs are evidence-based and highly effective for a wide range of conditions. Through secure video calls, we guide you through assessments and exercises, ensuring you perform them correctly.

Features:

  • Detailed Video Assessment.
  • Personalized Exercise App/Videos.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Whether you are a professional athlete or a weekend warrior, injuries can set you back. Dr. Nikhil's sports rehab programs focus on a safe and efficient return to sport. We treat conditions like ACL tears, rotator cuff injuries, ankle sprains, and tennis elbow.

Our approach combines manual therapy to reduce pain and inflammation with functional training to restore strength, agility, and power.

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Surgery is just the first step; rehabilitation is where recovery happens. We provide specialized protocols for:

  • Total Knee & Hip Replacements.
  • Ligament Reconstruction (ACL/PCL).
  • Spinal Surgeries (Discectomy, Laminectomy).
  • Fracture Fixations.

We work closely with your surgeon's protocols to ensure safe progression.

Pain Management Therapy

Chronic pain can be debilitating. We use a combination of modalities and therapeutic exercises to manage and alleviate pain conditions such as Chronic Back Pain, Cervical Spondylosis, Arthritis, and Fibromyalgia.

Our goal is to reduce dependency on painkillers and improve your functional ability through natural, physical methods.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Neurological conditions affect the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. We provide specialized therapy for Stroke (Paralysis), Parkinson's Disease, Spinal Cord Injuries, and Cerebral Palsy. Our focus is on neuroplasticity to help relearn lost functions.

Therapies Include:

  • Gait Training & Balance Exercises.
  • Constraint-Induced Movement Therapy (CIMT).
  • Functional Activity Training.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

We treat children with developmental delays, congenital conditions, and injuries. Our approach is playful yet effective, ensuring children engage in their therapy for better outcomes.

Conditions treated: Cerebral Palsy, Delayed Milestones, Torticollis, Club Foot, and Sports Injuries in children.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Aging gracefully means staying active and independent. We specialize in treating age-related conditions such as Osteoarthritis, Osteoporosis, and general weakness. Our fall prevention program is crucial for elderly safety.

Goals:

  • Improve Balance & prevent falls.
  • Maintain Joint Mobility.
  • Enhance Strength for daily activities.

Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation

Helping patients recover from heart and lung conditions. This includes post-CABG rehab, COPD management, and post-COVID breathing difficulties.

We teach breathing exercises, energy conservation techniques, and graded exercise programs to improve endurance.

Advanced Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Beyond basic pain relief, we offer advanced rehabilitation for complex orthopedic conditions. This includes rehab for multiple fractures, spinal deformities (Scoliosis/Kyphosis), and repetitive strain injuries.

Manual Therapy & Dry Needling

Hands-on techniques to mobilize joints and soft tissues. We also offer specialized Dry Needling therapy for trigger point release, which is highly effective for chronic muscle knots.

Techniques:

  • Joint Mobilization/Manipulation.
  • Myofascial Release.
  • Dry Needling & Cupping Therapy.
  • Kinesio Taping.
