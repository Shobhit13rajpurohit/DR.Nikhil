About Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit

Dedicated to restoring health and mobility through expert physiotherapy.

A Commitment to Healing

Professional Excellence & Compassionate Care

Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit is a distinguished physiotherapist practicing in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. With a strong academic background and extensive clinical experience, he has established himself as a trusted name in the field of rehabilitation.

Currently associated with the prestigious Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad, Dr. Nikhil handles a diverse range of complex cases, ranging from trauma recovery to chronic pain management. His exposure to a high-volume public hospital environment has honed his diagnostic skills and ability to design effective treatment plans for patients from all walks of life.

Dr. Nikhil believes in a holistic approach to physiotherapy. He understands that every patient is unique, and therefore, every treatment plan must be customized. His goal is not just to alleviate pain temporarily but to empower patients with the strength and knowledge to prevent future injuries.
