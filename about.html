About Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit

Dedicated to restoring health and mobility through expert physiotherapy.

A Commitment to Healing

Professional Excellence & Compassionate Care

Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit is a highly regarded physiotherapist based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, known for his clinical acumen and patient-centered approach. With a robust academic foundation and years of hands-on experience, Dr. Nikhil has successfully treated numerous patients, helping them regain independence and improve their quality of life.

Clinical Expertise: Currently serving as a Physiotherapist at Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad, Dr. Nikhil manages a wide spectrum of cases ranging from acute sports injuries and post-operative rehabilitation to chronic neurological disorders. This high-volume clinical environment has sharpened his diagnostic precision and ability to formulate evidence-based treatment protocols tailored to complex medical needs.

Private Practice Philosophy: Dr. Nikhil understands that healing is most effective in a comfortable environment. Through his exclusive Home Visit Services in Ahmedabad and global Online Consultations, he brings hospital-grade physiotherapy directly to patients. This model ensures personalized, one-on-one attention that is often difficult to achieve in busy clinical settings.

His holistic methodology goes beyond symptom relief; he focuses on identifying the root cause of dysfunction, correcting biomechanics, and empowering patients with the knowledge to prevent recurrence. Dr. Nikhil is dedicated to guiding you through every step of your recovery journey with empathy, expertise, and encouragement.

Clinical Experience

A journey of learning and professional growth.

Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit has built a robust clinical foundation through diverse experiences in top medical institutions. He is currently serving as a full-time Physiotherapist at Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad, since March 2024, where he specializes in injury rehabilitation and musculoskeletal care. Prior to this, he completed a rigorous internship as a Physiotherapy Assistant at the same institution.

His early career includes specialized internships in neurological and sports rehabilitation at Better Than Normal Clinic, Jaipur, and critical care management in OPD and ICU settings at Jeevan Rekha Super Speciality Hospital. He also gained extensive experience in orthopedic and gynecological physiotherapy during his tenure at Bhatia Hospital, Jaipur.
