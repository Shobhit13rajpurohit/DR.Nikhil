About Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit

Dedicated to restoring health and mobility through expert physiotherapy.

A Commitment to Healing

Professional Excellence & Compassionate Care

Dr. Nikhil Rajpurohit is a distinguished physiotherapist practicing in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. With a strong academic background and extensive clinical experience, he has established himself as a trusted name in the field of rehabilitation.

Currently serving as a Physiotherapist at Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad, Dr. Nikhil handles a diverse range of complex cases, from trauma recovery to chronic pain management. His exposure to a high-volume public hospital environment has honed his diagnostic skills and ability to design effective treatment plans.

Note on Practice: While Dr. Nikhil is associated with Civil Hospital, his private services are exclusively Home Visits in Ahmedabad (Selected Areas) and Online Consultations. He brings the clinic's expertise directly to your doorstep, ensuring you receive personalized care in the comfort of your home.

Dr. Nikhil believes in a holistic approach, customizing every treatment plan to the unique needs of the patient to not just alleviate pain but prevent future injuries.
