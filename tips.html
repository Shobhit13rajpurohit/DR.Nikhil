Physiotherapy Tips & Exercises

Expert advice to keep you moving pain-free.

Daily Health Tips

Small changes in your routine can make a big difference.

Ergonomics

Ensure your computer screen is at eye level to prevent neck strain. Keep your feet flat on the floor.

Text Neck

Avoid looking down at your phone for long periods. Bring the phone up to eye level.

Sleep Posture

Use a pillow that supports the natural curve of your neck. Avoid sleeping on your stomach.

Hydration

Staying hydrated helps maintain the elasticity of your soft tissues and prevents cramps.

Simple Home Exercises

Try these simple stretches to relieve tension. (Consult a doctor if you have pain).

Neck Stretches

Great for relieving tension from office work.

  1. Sit upright with shoulders relaxed.
  2. Gently tilt your right ear towards your right shoulder.
  3. Hold for 15-30 seconds.
  4. Repeat on the left side.

Back Extensions

Helps counteract slouching.

  1. Stand with your hands on your lower back.
  2. Gently arch backward, looking up at the ceiling.
  3. Hold for 5 seconds and return to neutral.
  4. Repeat 10 times.
